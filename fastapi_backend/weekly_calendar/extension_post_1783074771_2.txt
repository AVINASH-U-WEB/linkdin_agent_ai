"AI Agent... AI Agent..." nu ellarum pesitu irukanga. 😅

But enna da adhu actually pannuthu?

Many people think:
AI Agent = Automation
But I see it differently.

Simple ah sonna...
AI Agent = AI + Action 🚀

You give a task.
It doesn't just give an answer.

It can:
→ Understand the task
→ Decide the next step
→ Use tools
→ Take action
→ Complete the workflow
That's what makes AI Agents different.

For example...
I wanted to post consistently on LinkedIn.

Every day I had the same questions:
❌ What should I post?
❌ How do I research it?
❌ How do I write it?
❌ Is this good enough to publish?

So I built an AI Agent for my LinkedIn content workflow.

Now it helps me:
✅ Find content ideas
✅ Research topics
✅ Write drafts
✅ Improve content

✅ Ask for approval before posting

The final decision is still mine.

I review it.
I approve it.
I post it. 😎

So technically...
Is this an Avinash post?
Or an AI Agent post? 😂

Maybe both.

AI didn't replace my work.
It removed the repetitive work.

Now I spend more time building products, learning new things, and sharing my experiences. That's why I'm excited about AI Agents. Not because they do everything.

But because they help us focus on what matters.
